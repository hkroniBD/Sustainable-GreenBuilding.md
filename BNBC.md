## üîê 3. Earthing & Grounding Requirements

Earthing is one of the most safety-critical topics in BNBC.

### BNBC Discusses

* Earthing conductor sizes
* Earthing electrode types (pipes, plates, rods)
* Resistance requirements
* Equipment grounding vs system grounding
* Bonding of metallic parts
* Earthing pit design guidelines

### Why Important

Earthing provides a low-resistance path for fault current, preventing shock and reducing the risk of fire.

## üî• 4. Protection Against Overcurrent, Short-Circuit, and Shock

BNBC provides selection rules for protection equipment.

### Includes

* MCB, MCCB, fuse ratings
* Short-circuit calculation basics
* RCD/RCCB requirements
* Arc fault protection
* Isolation and switching rules

### Engineering Meaning

Protection devices must clear faults faster than the cable reaches dangerous temperatures.

A simple concept behind these rules:
`Fault Current = Voltage / Fault Impedance`

BNBC ensures protection devices operate within safe margins.
